About

SwimTime uses an ultrasonic sensor to aid swimmers get closer to their goals. Attached on one end of the pool, it detects when you finished your lap and automatically records your lap times. Through a user interface you can control this IoT module and also track your performance over time.

Find out more about the solution

Solution

How to use SwimTime?

All the user must do is specify through the website how many laps they are planning to do. A couple seconds thereafter the timer begins. The ultrasonic sensor measures the distance to the swimmer by transmitting and receiving sound waves and can detect when the swimmer has finished their lap. At that moment, SwimTime records the lap time, sends the data to the website and resets the timer for the next lap. This repeats until the swimmer finishes all the laps specified. Product Features

Connect to the Internet

The connection of the board to the internet facilitates the flow of information between the module and the website. The module receives the number of laps you specified on the website before you start swimming and sends the lap times back to the website when you are done.





Automatically Measure Your Lap-Times

Equipped with an ultrasonic sensor, the board can detect when you have finished one lap by sending high frequency sound waves and timing how long it took for the echo to arrive back.

Track Your Performance

After you are done with your session you can visit the website to see how well you performed. Over time you can track your improvement by analysing the change in your overall times and by extracting information about your stamina from the individual lap times.






User-Friendly Interface

The simple yet effective user interface means you can focus on your swimming and let SwimTime do the rest.
